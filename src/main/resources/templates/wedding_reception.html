Roaring 20's Theme

About the Theme

The bride and groom are happy to announce that we are putting on a Roaring 20's themed wedding. Seacrets is decorating the reception hall to match the theme and the wedding party is dressing to match the style. Wedding guests are highly encouraged to participate in the them and dress according to the style, although we wish to make clear this is not a requirement for attendance.
